Song Name Description

"Rich Flex" is a song by Canadian rapper Drake and Atlanta-based rapper 21 Savage. It was released on November 4, 2022, as the opening track from their collaborative studio album Her Loss. The song interpolates "Savage" (2020) by Megan Thee Stallion, "24's" (2003) by T.I., and "Red Opps" (2016) by 21 Savage, as well as sampling "I Want You, Girl" (1973) by Sugar and "Nora's Transformation" (1973) by Charles Bernstein.

"Hotline Bling" is a song recorded by Canadian rapper Drake, which serves as the lead single from his fourth studio album Views (2016). The song is credited as a bonus track on the album.[1][2] It was made available for digital download on July 31, 2015, through Cash Money, Young Money and Republic.

"God's Plan" is a song recorded by Canadian rapper Drake, released on January 19, 2018, through Young Money and Cash Money. The song was written by Drake, Brock Korsan, and producers Cardo, Yung Exclusive, Boi-1da and Noah "40" Shebib. It acts as a single from his second EP, Scary Hours (2018), and the lead single from his fifth studio album, Scorpion (2018). [1][2][3] Musically, it has been described as pop, pop-rap, and trap, with lyrics addressing Drake's fame and fate.
